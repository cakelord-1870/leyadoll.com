Gloveleya, also known as “Spring Girl”, is every baby’s dream toy to have. With her signature smile, embroidered flower dress, and ultra-soft features, it is without question this is the perfect doll for your baby to cuddle, play and grow up with.  

Craft

Size: 16.5 inches (43cm) in standing height and 8oz (220g) in weight. We strategically designed the doll’s arms and legs to be no bigger than 4cm, which is the perfect size for babies to grab and play with easily.

You can be confident in knowing that we develop our dolls with extremely proper care. During production, we ensure every seam is tight and impeccably stitched with cross-stitching on the inside to withstand your baby’s cute aggression.

Personalized name dolls

One of the best features of our Gloveleya doll is that you have the ability to personalize her! Using up to 12 characters, you can place a name or any inscription to give the doll a unique identity. The material we use for lettering is safe and skin-friendly, just like the rest of the doll’s materials. Even with proper handwashings, the letters are durable enough to maintain color and sharpness.

Safety

Our Gloveleya dolls are uniquely crafted with high-quality environmental materials for lasting value. We create our dolls out of soft polyester fibers, skin-friendly plush and fill them with high elasticity PP cotton.  With this perfect combination of materials, our dolls have the ability to stay soft and warm while being fluorescent, kneading, and fragrance-free.

As parents, you know very well that kids tend to be messy.  That is why we designed our dolls to be completely washable to save you the hassle. We provided you with everything you need to know about washing care on the doll’s tag for easy reference.
We know the importance of safety, especially when it comes to your little one. We manufacture Gloveleya dolls from the best and safest materials that comply with the applicable safety standards. We have successfully completed numerous safety tests involving compliancy with children's essential health, safety, and environmental requirements. The tests we have passed are US-CPSIA, ASTM F963-16/-17, CA-CCPSA, EU-CE, EN71-1-2-3, and GB6675.1-4.
