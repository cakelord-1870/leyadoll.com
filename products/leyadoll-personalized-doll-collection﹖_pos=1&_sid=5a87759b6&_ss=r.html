Safety

We know the importance of safety, especially when it comes to your little one. We manufacture Leyadoll from the best and safest materials that comply with the applicable safety standards. Materials

Our Leyadolls are uniquely crafted with high-quality environmental materials for lasting value. We create our dolls out of soft polyester fibers, skin-friendly plush, and fill them with high elasticity PP cotton.  With this perfect combination of materials, our dolls have the ability to stay soft and warm while being fluorescent, kneading, and fragrance-free.


As parents, you know very well that kids tend to be messy. That is why we designed our dolls to be completely machine-washable to save you the hassle. We provided you with everything you need to know about washing care on the doll’s tag for easy reference.
